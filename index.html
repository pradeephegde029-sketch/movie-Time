FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How do I book a ticket? ▼
Select your seats on Step 1, fill in your details on Step 2, pay via UPI on Step 3, upload the payment screenshot, enter the UTR and submit. You'll receive a digital ticket with a QR code instantly.
How do free tickets work? ▼
If free tickets are available you'll see an "Access Code" panel on Step 1. Enter the code given to you by the organisers to unlock the free rate. Your total will drop to ₹0 — no payment or screenshot needed.
Why does the price show ₹100 even though free tickets exist? ▼
Free tickets are access-code controlled. The price shows the regular rate until you verify your code. Once verified, the price drops to FREE automatically.
Will my booking disappear after refresh? ▼
Never. All data is stored permanently in Supabase. Refreshing always reloads your tickets fresh from the database — nothing is ever lost.
When will my ticket be approved? ▼
Admin verifies payment and updates the status. The app auto-refreshes every 10 seconds so you'll see changes in real-time under "My Tickets".
Can I book multiple seats? ▼
Yes — use the + / − buttons on Step 1 to select up to the max allowed per booking (set by admin, usually 5).
What if my booking is rejected? ▼
If the screenshot is unclear or the UTR doesn't match, admin may reject it. You can book again with the correct details.
